What is an Election?

An election is a formal decision-making process in which eligible citizens of a country choose individuals to represent them in government or to decide on important issues. Elections are essential for the functioning of a democracy, allowing the people to have a voice in who governs them and how they are governed. In the Philippines, elections are held for various levels of government, including the presidential, senatorial, congressional, and local elections.

2025 National and Local Elections

The 2025 National and Local Elections in the Philippines are set to take place on May 12, 2025. These elections will be crucial for determining the direction of the country’s future, as citizens will vote for positions such as president, senators, congressmen, governors, and mayors. The importance of informed voting cannot be overstated, as it shapes the nation’s governance and impacts the lives of all Filipinos.
